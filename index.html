Floorplanner — help

Getting started

  • Click + Add room, then drag on the canvas to draw a room.
  • Click + Add item ▾ to place furniture (rectangles, circles, and doors).
  • Click anything to select it; edit exact dimensions in the right sidebar.
  • Use Share link to copy a self-contained URL, or Export to download JSON.

Canvas navigation

  • Mouse wheel or trackpad pinch: zoom at cursor.
  • Drag an empty area of the canvas: pan.
  • Middle-drag or Space+drag: pan.
  • Three-finger drag (macOS Accessibility) or 3-finger touch: pan.

Start a new plan?
